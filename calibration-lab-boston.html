About Us

Transcat's Boston Massachusetts Calibration Lab

  • Accredited calibrations to ISO/IEC 17025:2017
  • Several service options available
  • Pickup and delivery throughout Massachusetts
  • Calibration of all major leading manufacturers
  • In-lab and onsite services available
  • Consistent lab processes with CalTrak Metrology System

Transcat’s Boston calibration laboratory is located in Wilmington, MA just 30 minutes from Boston and New Hampshire. Our laboratory is ISO/IEC 17025:2017 Accredited and provides local depot and onsite calibration services to the surrounding area.
